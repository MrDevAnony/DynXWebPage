How to activate the profile on your device:
- Download *dynx*.mobileconfig and "Allow"
- Install the configuration profile: Settings > Profile loaded
- Confirm the "Network traffic" warning and press "Install"
- The last installed profile is automatically active (General > VPN & Network > DNS)
- Airplane mode must be switched on and off once to clear the cache
